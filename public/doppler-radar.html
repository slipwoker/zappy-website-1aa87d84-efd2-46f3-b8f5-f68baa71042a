How Our Doppler Radar Keeps Oneida Safe

At WYNC NEWS, our dedicated weather team transforms raw radar data into life-saving local news coverage. From initial signal detection to community-wide severe weather alerts, every step is engineered for accuracy and speed.

1

Radar Data Collection

Our advanced Doppler radar system continuously scans the atmosphere across Central New York, detecting precipitation intensity, wind velocity, and storm rotation. This real-time data forms the foundation of every forecast we deliver.

2

Expert Team Interpretation

Our certified meteorologists analyze radar signatures, satellite imagery, and atmospheric models. They identify developing threats such as supercells, microbursts, and heavy snow bands, translating complex data into actionable insights.

3

Localized Forecasting

We refine global models to the hyper-local level for Oneida and surrounding communities. By factoring in local topography and lake-effect dynamics, we produce precise, neighborhood-specific forecasts you can trust.

4

Urgent Community Communication

When severe weather threatens, our team issues immediate alerts across broadcast, digital, and social platforms. We ensure every Oneida resident receives critical warnings with the urgency and clarity needed to stay safe.
